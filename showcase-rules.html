Official Terms and Conditions

1. Eligibility

  • Open to individuals aged 18+ worldwide
  • Must have a legitimate OpenClaw project to submit
  • One submission per person per month
  • Employees, contractors, and immediate family of OpenClaw are not eligible

2. How to Enter

  • Submit your project through the official showcase submission form
  • Include: project name, description, working URL, and screenshot
  • Submission must be your own original work
  • Deadline: 25th of each month

3. Voting Period

  • Voting runs from the 26th through the last day of each month
  • One vote per person (tracked via browser/localStorage)
  • Votes cannot be changed once submitted
  • Attempting to manipulate votes will result in disqualification

4. Winner Selection

  • Winner is determined by highest number of likes at voting deadline
  • In case of a tie, the earliest submission wins
  • Winner announced within 3 business days after voting ends

5. The Prize

  • $50 USD Amazon Gift Card (or equivalent)
  • Delivered digitally via email within 7 days of winner announcement
  • Prize is non-transferable, non-refundable
  • No cash alternative offered

6. Disqualification

  • Submission contains harmful, illegal, or inappropriate content
  • Violates third-party intellectual property rights
  • Manipulates voting system
  • Provides false information during submission
  • Attempts to spam or abuse the platform

7. Rights & Ownership

  • You retain full ownership of your submitted project
  • By submitting, you grant permission to feature your project on OpenClawdemy
  • You may withdraw your submission at any time by contacting us

8. Limitation of Liability

  • Contest provided "as is" without warranties
  • Not responsible for technical issues affecting submissions or voting
  • Not responsible for lost, late, or misdirected entries

9. Privacy

  • Only your name and project info are displayed publicly
  • Email used for prize delivery only
  • Data is not sold to third parties

10. Changes to Contest

  • OpenClaw reserves the right to modify, suspend, or cancel the contest
  • Participants will be notified of significant changes
